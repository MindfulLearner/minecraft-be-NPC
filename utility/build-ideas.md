# Build Ideas — Friends Server

## NPC Village / Hub Area
- Central spawn plaza with paths leading to each NPC
- Individual small buildings for each NPC (fisherman's shack near water, hunter's lodge in forest, builder's workshop, decorator's cottage)
- Quest board with signs listing active quests
- Torch-lit streets between buildings
- Welcome arch at spawn

---

## Economy & Storage
- Market stalls for Mr Francesco (glass front, counter, item frames with prices)
- Community chest room (sorted by category: food, tools, building blocks)
- Gold nugget vault — decorative room where players store earnings
- Donation chest near spawn for new players

---

## Functional Farms
- Wheat / carrot / potato farm (for quests and food supply)
- Tree farm — oak rows for taglialegna quest
- Mob grinder — skeleton farm for ossa quest, zombie farm for Elena
- Chicken farm — automated with hoppers for Elena's quest
- Cow pen — fenced area near Elena

## Fishing Spot (for Old Piero)
- Wooden dock extending over a lake or river
- Small boat tied to the dock
- Lanterns along the pier
- Old Piero's abandoned house nearby — broken windows, overgrown, sad vibe
